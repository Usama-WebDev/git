S.CONFIRM Table Water

Welcome to S.CONFIRM Table Water — Online Distribution

This is a simple front-end demo of an Online Table Water Distribution Management System. Use the navigation to try Customer, Vendor/Admin, or Delivery views.

How to use

  1. Register or login from any page (system stores users in your browser).
  2. As a Customer: place orders and view your orders.
  3. As Vendor/Admin: view all orders, assign delivery person, update status.
  4. As Delivery: view assigned orders and mark as delivered.
